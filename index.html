* C L Á U D I A
B A R R O S

Cláudia Barros is as curious as one can be and doesn't know how to sit still. Around her discoveries in the design world, she ends up falling in love with a variety of areas, from analogue to digital. If she isn't with her head on designing, she's cooking up something else on the oven. Cláudia likes to let herself be carried away by people's enthusiasmand takes a little piece of each person, each place, for herself. Always ready for the next great adventure!
